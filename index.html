mahalakshmi silks

Mahalekshmi Silks is a leading name in the textile & apparel retail trade in Kerala. Established in 2012 with a showroom in Thiruvalla, Mahalekshmi Silks has expanded to Muthoor & Ettumanoor within a short period. Consequently, Mahalekshmi silks has become the one-stop destination for all your fashion and style needs and in turn the fashion destination of Central Kerala.We pride ourselves on being the “Genuine Silk people” as our logo states. The sheer variety of silk sarees available at our showrooms will delight & dazzle you. We have an eclectic collection of Kancheepuram, Banarasi, Paithani, Pochampalli, Soft Silk Sarees; You name it, We have it. We source our silks directly from weavers in weaving villages at Kancheepuram, Peelikothi, Banaras, etc many of whom weave sarees exclusively for Mahalekshmi silks. This gives us unique designs not found in any other silk showrooms. Draped in an elegant Silk saree from our collection, you will be the cynosure of every eye anywhere you go! In addition, our Saree collection includes exclusive designer sarees incorporating the latest design & fashion trends which will make you stand out in any event or function when you are wrapped in them. Cotton Silk, Gadwal, Linen, the choice of saree materials and designs are endless.
